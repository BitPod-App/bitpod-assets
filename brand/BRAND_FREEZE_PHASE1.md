# Brand Freeze — Phase 1

Phase 1 of migration keeps brand assets in a controlled placeholder state.

## Freeze rules

- No logo redesign in Phase 1.
- No destructive renaming/deleting of starter assets.
- Social preview cards may be added/updated for operational visibility only.
- Current corrected assets are valid for Phase 1 usage but are **not** final canonical vector master assets.

## Why

This keeps migration execution stable while avoiding visual drift.

## Deferred to Phase 2

- naming normalization
- export matrix policy
- Figma import/export runbook
- visual identity refinements
- true canonical vector redraw and final editable master logo set
