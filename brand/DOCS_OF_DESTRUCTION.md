# Docs of Destruction

Status: Preserved on purpose
Date: 2026-04-10

## Why this file exists

The BitPod brand cleanup should not sanitize the record.

The existing Phase 1 brand documents are valuable precisely because they describe, in plain language, how the repo ended up sheltering a broken pseudo-system: a package that knew it was not canonical, yet still behaved as if it might become one.

That failure mode should remain visible.

## What is being preserved

The following documents remain part of the historical record:

- `brand/README.md`
- `brand/asset-provenance.md`
- `brand/BRAND_FREEZE_PHASE1.md`
- `brand/guidelines/brand-guidelines.md`

They are not being preserved because they are good canon.
They are being preserved because they are good evidence.

## Reading rule

Read them as:

- testimony
- containment notes
- recovery-era documents
- proof of where process and artifact truth diverged

Do **not** read them as final authority for the BitPod logo system.

## Permanent epitaph

> Here lay the semi preserved remains of the catastrophe in life and death. The ruins of a man whose profession was to manage products, but only managed to provide a stable stone on which to house a decosystem, a solid rock on which house the ghost incoherence and scattered whispers unable to voice the very promise this stronghold portends to pass on to posterity.

## Practical consequence

The catastrophe record stays.

The canonical authority moves elsewhere.
